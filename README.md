# 📚 PU Undergraduate Entry Test Preparation

Welcome to the **PU Undergraduate Entry Test Preparation** repository.

This repository is a small contribution from my side for all the upcoming students who are preparing for the Punjab University Undergraduate Entry Test and similar entrance exams.

---

## 🌟 About Me

I am a **BS IT graduate from PUCIT (Punjab University College of Information Technology)**.

I appeared in the PU Undergraduate Entry Test, successfully secured admission, completed my BS IT degree, and Alhamdulillah, I have now graduated.

During my preparation journey, I relied heavily on structured notes, past papers, and helping material — so I created this repository to give back and help future students.

---

## 🎯 Purpose of This Repository

This repo is specially created for **junior students and future aspirants** who are:

- Preparing for PU Undergraduate Entry Test
- Looking for organized study material
- Searching for past papers and test patterns
- Needing quick and reliable resources in one place

My goal is to make your preparation easier, more structured, and less stressful.

---

## 📁 What You Will Find Here

- 📖 Entry Test Preparation Books (PU & Dogar material)
- 📝 Sample Past Papers
- 📊 Test Patterns & Syllabi
- 📚 Helping Notes & Study Material
- 📂 Useful Additional Resources

---

## 🚀 How to Use This Repo

1. Go through the folders based on your subject/need  
2. Start with test patterns to understand the exam structure  
3. Practice past papers regularly  
4. Use books & notes to strengthen your concepts  
5. Stay consistent — that’s the key

---

## 💬 A Message to Juniors

If you are just starting your journey, don’t panic.

I was once in your place — confused, unsure, and figuring everything out step by step. With consistency and the right material, it becomes manageable.

Believe in your effort, stay focused, and trust the process.  
InshaAllah, you will get there too.
